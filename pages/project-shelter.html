Survival Horror Prototype

Project Shelter

A systemic survival game prototype set in a post-apocalyptic environment, focusing on tension, resource management, and strategic base defense.

Key Highlights

  • Modular Base: Manage and upgrade a hub for repairs and storage.
  • Persistent Scarcity: Every bullet and scrap counts across sessions.
  • Reactive AI: Stealth-based encounters with dynamic enemy cycles.

Core Systems

Gathering

Scavenge vital crafting components and supplies scattered throughout a dangerous world.

Weaponry

Loadout management focused on weapon durability and limited resource adaptation.

Enemy AI

Finite state machine AI that dictates patrol, chase, and search behaviors based on environmental stimuli.
